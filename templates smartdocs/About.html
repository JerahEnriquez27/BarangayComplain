About SmartDocs System

The SmartDocs System is an online platform that lets users submit document requests quickly without having to visit a government office physically.

Purpose:
• Enable users to request documents quickly and conveniently.
• Assist officials in managing and processing requests efficiently.
• Enhance communication and transparency within the system.

Users can submit document requests anytime, and officials can track and process them promptly, ensuring faster and more organized service.
