About Barangay Portal

About the Barangay Complaint System

The Barangay Complaint System is an online platform that allows residents to easily send their complaints and concerns to the barangay. It is designed to help people report problems happening in their community, such as noise disturbances, garbage issues, damaged facilities, neighborhood conflicts, or any situation that needs barangay attention.

This system makes the complaint process faster and more convenient. Instead of physically going to the barangay hall, lining up, or filling out manual forms, residents can submit their complaints anytime and anywhere using a computer or mobile device. It helps the barangay respond more quickly because the complaints are organized, recorded, and received in real time.

Through this system, residents can feel more confident that their concerns are heard, properly documented, and monitored until they are resolved. It promotes transparency, faster communication, and better service for the community.

Objectives of the System

To give residents an easy way to report complaints
To make sure problems are answered quickly
To improve communication between residents and barangay officials
To keep records of all complaints for proper tracking
To make the complaint process faster and more organized

How It Works

1.Register or Log In – Create an account or sign in to access the system.
2.Submit a Complaint – Fill in your complaint details and send it.
3.Review – Barangay officials check and verify the complaint.
4.Action – The barangay works on solving the issue.
5.Status Update – Residents can check the complaint’s status until it is resolved.
