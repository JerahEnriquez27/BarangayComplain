The Barangay Complaint System is an online platform that lets residents quickly send complaints to the barangay without visiting the hall.

Purpose:
• Make reporting community issues fast and easy.
• Help the barangay manage and respond to complaints efficiently.
• Improve communication and transparency in the community.
Residents can submit complaints anytime, and barangay officials can track and respond quickly, ensuring problems are solved faster.
